Welcome to our International Catholic Community!

The beginnings of this English-speaking Catholic Community date back to September 2013. Those who had until then participated in the celebrations of the American Military Catholic Community made plans to fill the gap that was left when the American Forces left Heidelberg.

In September 2013, we celebrated our first Mass in English language in the Church of St. Michael in Heidelberg's Südstadt. Since then, every Sunday at 1p.m., the community gathers to celebrate Holy Mass, as of July 2025 in St. Bartholomäus in Wieblingen. Bringing together a people from all over the world, the community serves Catholic faithful from far and near; from established families to expatriate workers of international companies, exchange students and tourists who find themselves in the area. Despite their many different backgrounds, cultures and languages, our community members are united through the bonds of their faith.

Apart from our Sunday Mass, we offer regular occasions to receive the Sacrament of Reconciliation. Of course, the celebration of other sacraments is possible as well. Once a month, the community meets for a Social Gathering where we interact with one another over some fingerfood.

ICCH is part of the Catholic City Church of Heidelberg ("Stadtkirche Heidelberg"). As a community whose activities are organized by volunteers, it depends on the help of everyone present.

A small but growing and active community, we are always happy to welcome guests and newcomers!
